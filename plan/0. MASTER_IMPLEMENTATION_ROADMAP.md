## Executive Summary

OpsManager is an enterprise-grade infrastructure management and automation platform designed to provide unified control over servers, incidents, and operational workflows with a focus on Site Reliability Engineering (SRE) principles. This master roadmap outlines the complete implementation strategy across 8 core modules, transforming the platform from concept to production-ready system.

### Platform Vision
**"A unified, intelligent operations platform that empowers IT teams to manage, monitor, automate, and secure their entire infrastructure with confidence and efficiency."**

### Business Objectives
1. **Reduce MTTR (Mean Time To Resolution)** by 60% through automated incident response
2. **Improve Infrastructure Visibility** with real-time server monitoring and observability
3. **Enhance Security Posture** with comprehensive audit logging and RBAC
4. **Scale Operations** supporting 1,000+ managed servers and 1,000+ concurrent users
5. **Ensure Compliance** with SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR requirements
6. **SRE Excellence** with SLO tracking, error budgets, and automated runbooks

### Technical Highlights
- **Microservices Architecture:** Modular, scalable, independently deployable
- **Cloud-Native Design:** Kubernetes-ready, horizontal scaling, zero-downtime deployments
- **Security-First:** Zero-trust architecture, encryption at rest and in transit
- **Real-Time Operations:** WebSocket-based live updates, sub-second response times
- **API-First:** Comprehensive REST APIs with OpenAPI documentation

### Technical Risks
| Risk | Impact | Probability | Mitigation |
